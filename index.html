Summary

I am currently a post-doctorate researcher at SC (Signals and Communication) team of IRIT where I work on tensor decomposition under the supervision of Henrique Goulart and Cédric Févotte.

I graduated in 2015 from Télécom SudParis in France and National Chiao Tung University in Taiwan, under the supervision of Professor Shiao-Li Tsao. My research interest was on GPU architecture and rendering algorithms.

Then, from 2017 to 2020, I studied PhD under the supervision of Professor Jean-Christophe Pesquet and Professor Marc Castella with the collaboration of Laurent Duval. I worked on polynomial and rational optimization applied to signal processing problems.
